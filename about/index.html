About Us

We have over 30 years experience in picture framing

With a combined experience of 30 years framing, our framers offer the highest quality bespoke framing service to conservation and museum standards.

Top quality products are used to ensure the best presentation and protection of your artwork.

We have an extensive range of both hand-finished and manufactured mouldings.

All our mounts are acid-free.

We offer 3 different types of glass – standard clear glass, conservation glass (uv protection) and museum glass (uv protection, non-reflective).

We frame everything from oil paintings, watercolours, pastels, photographs, prints, sports’ memorabilia, books, certificates, children’s art, tapestries, wedding dresses etc

Please do not hesitate to contact us to discuss your framing requirements.
